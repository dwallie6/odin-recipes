Slow Cooker Creamed Corn

A recipe for my favorite side: creamed corn

Seriously, I don't know how throwing cream and corn together is this good.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces frozen corn kernels
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine corn, cream cheese, butter, milk, and sugar in a slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Cook on Low for 4 to 6 hours or High for 2 to 4 hours.
